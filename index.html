About the Institute

The AKSUM CHRONICLE is a premier digital repository dedicated to the preservation and scholarly study of African, Mediterranean, and Middle Eastern civilizations. Our mission is to provide unprecedented access to rare manuscripts, primary sources, and academic research spanning millennia of human achievement.

Founded on principles of rigorous scholarship and institutional excellence, we serve as a bridge between ancient wisdom and contemporary knowledge, ensuring that the legacy of Aksumite, Byzantine, and Ethiopian civilizations remains accessible to scholars worldwide.
